Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredients

Method

2 2/3 cups flour

120 g unsalted butter

1 cup brown sugar (packed)

1 egg

1/3 cup molasses/honney

1/2 tsp each of cinnamon, ginger, allspice, nutmeg, cloves

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 tsp baking soda

1 tsp vanilla paste

Wet ingredients: mix butter and sugar until creamy and smooth. Add egg, honey and vanilla and incorporate it well.

Dry ingredients: to the flour add the salt, baking soda, spices and mix to incorporate all the ingredients. Add the dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until incorporated.

Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and chill for 1.5 h.

Roll and cut the dough in desired shapes. Bake in preheated oven at 350 F for 10-15 minuntes.

Decorate with icing once cooled completely.

Blueberry pancakes

Ingredients

Method

3 eggs

10 tbsp flour

6 tbsp greek yogurt

6 tbsp whole milk

1/4 tsp vanilla paste

1 tsp melted butter/sunflower oil

a pinch of salt

maple syrup to serve

Separate egg whites from yolk. Mix whites until fluffy.

Dry ingredients: to the flour add the salt, baking soda, spices and mix to incorporate all the ingredients. Add the dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until incorporated.

Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and chill for 1.5 h.

Roll and cut the dough in desired shapes. Bake in preheated oven at 350 F for 10-15 minuntes.

Decorate with icing once cooled completely.
