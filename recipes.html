Gingerbread Cookies

Ingredients

Method

2 2/3 cups flour

120 g unsalted butter

1 cup brown sugar (packed)

1 egg

1/3 cup molasses/honney

1/2 tsp each of cinnamon, ginger, allspice, nutmeg, cloves

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 tsp baking soda

1 tsp vanilla paste

Method

Step 1

Wet ingredients: mix butter and sugar until creamy and smooth. Add egg, honey and vanilla and incorporate it well.

Step 2

Dry ingredients: to the flour add the salt, baking soda, spices and mix to incorporate all the ingredients. Add the dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until incorporated.

Step 3

Wrap the dough in plastic wrap & chill for 1.5 h.

Step 4

Roll and cut the dough in desired shapes. Bake in preheated oven at 350 F for 10-15 minuntes. Let them cool completely o na wire rack and decorate with icing.

Blueberry Pancakes

Ingredients

Method

3 eggs

10 tbsp flour

6 tbsp greek yogurt

6 tbsp whole milk

1/4 tsp vanilla paste

1 tsp melted butter/sunflower oil

a pinch of salt

maple syrup to serve

Method

Step 1

Separate egg whites from yolks. Mix whites until fluffy.

Step 2

To the egg yolks att the rest of the ingredients and mix until they are throughly incorporated. Now add the egg whites and mix well.

Step 3

Cook pancakes in a nonstick skillet on medium heat. Serve with maple syrup and fresh fruit of your choice.

Banana Bread

Ingredients

Method

2 cups all-purpose flour

2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

3/4 cup brown sugar

2 eggs

1/2 cup butter

1/4 tsp salt

1 tsp baking soda

1 tsp vanilla paste

Method

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

Step 2

Wet ingredients: cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.

Step 3

Dry ingredients: combine flour, baking soda and salt. Stir into the banana mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.

Step 4

Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let banana bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
